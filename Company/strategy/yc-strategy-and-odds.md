# Critical Success Factors

## Before Sending Demo

**Confirm these three things (multiplies your odds):**

1. **Co-founder Status**
   - Is the co-founder official and committed?
   - Both available to drop out if YC accepts?
   - Document this commitment

2. **Farmer Conversations**
   - Have you talked to 3-5 actual growers/agronomists?
   - What specific problems do they mention?
   - Get quotes to reference in follow-up

3. **Lab Partnership**
   - Do you have a collaboration letter from a professor?
   - Which lab? Which institution?
   - Timeline for first experiments?

### Odds Multiplier
- **All three confirmed:** 30-40% chance
- **Co-founder + farmers:** 15-25%
- **Without clarity:** 5-10%

### Post-Demo Follow-Up
If they ask for more info, immediately provide:
- Farmer quotes and email threads
- Co-founder bio and commitment letter
- Professor letter of intent for lab access
- Timeline for first wet-lab round

## Key Insights

### Why IG Convergence Matters Most
A heatmap showing 10,000 sequences with bright cluster at binding region is:
- ✅ **Quantitative** (not anecdotal)
- ✅ **Reproducible** (not lucky one-offs)
- ✅ **Scientifically sound** (proves real learning)
- ✅ **Justifies your strategy** (positions converge → locking works)

**Time to produce:** 30 minutes analysis + 5 minutes visualization
**Potential impact:** Moves demo from "promising predictions" to "proven convergence"
