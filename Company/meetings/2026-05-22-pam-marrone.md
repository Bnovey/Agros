## Her core message

> "No way to get an LOI without wet lab data."

She was direct: companies need to see **efficacy data** and a credible **manufacturing path** before any deal. Specifically:
- **Efficacy spectrum** — what does it kill, at what concentration, against what organisms
- **Manufacturing** — how would this be produced at scale (fermentation? synthesis?)

## On Botrytis

**Do not target Botrytis cinerea as a primary pitch.** She flagged it as a "post-disease" problem — growers typically discover it after the fact, making prevention-based biologicals a harder sell. The market is also crowded.
