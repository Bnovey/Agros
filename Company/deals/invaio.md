## What They Want

Peptide candidates from Agros's generative pipeline to test in vitro on their end. They do the wet lab validation; we supply the candidates.

This is a **candidate supply / pipeline access deal** — not a co-development or platform licensing deal (yet).

---

## What Agros Brings

- BoltzGen + Boltz-2 generative pipeline producing novel peptide candidates against specified targets
- IG analysis giving per-residue attribution — tells them *why* a candidate binds, enabling faster iteration
- Target-agnostic platform: can point at any target they specify
- Speed: weeks from target to ranked candidate list, vs. months of traditional screening

**Why not just use Peptyde Bio's pipeline?** Peptyde Bio gave Invaio an IP portfolio of *known* peptides. Agros gives them an engine to generate *new* ones — against any target, with interpretable design rationale.
