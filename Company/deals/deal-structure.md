# Agros Deal Structure — Tiered Pipeline License

Framework for commercial agreements with Invaio Sciences and Innatrix. All deals follow the same tiered structure — pricing varies by partner based on backing and resources.

---

## Model Overview

Agros delivers batches of ~100 ranked peptide candidates (sequences + IG attribution reports) for a specified target. The partner handles synthesis and testing. Rights to advance candidates into further stages of development require a relicense at each tier.

**Agros retains all platform IP.** Candidates are licensed, not sold. The generative pipeline, IG analysis methodology, and model weights remain Agros's.

---

## License Tiers

### Tier 1 — Initial Batch License
**What's delivered:** ~100 ranked peptide sequences for one target, with per-residue IG attribution reports and binding affinity predictions from Boltz-2.

**Rights granted:** In vivo bioassay screening only. Partner may synthesize candidates and run in vivo efficacy assays. No rights to advance any candidate beyond initial screening.

**Exclusivity:** Per-target exclusivity for 12 months — Agros will not license the same batch or a substantially similar batch for the same target to a direct competitor during this window.

| Partner | Tier 1 Fee |
|---|---|
| Invaio Sciences | **$150K** |
| Innatrix | **$75K** |

---

### Tier 2 — Field Trial License
**Trigger:** Partner elects to advance a candidate from in vivo screening into efficacy field trials (small-plot or greenhouse field conditions).

**Rights granted:** Field trial rights for the advancing candidate(s). Partner may conduct multi-site field trials and generate EPA submission data.

**Priced per advancing candidate** — if 5 candidates advance from a batch of 100, 5 relicenses are required.

| Partner | Tier 2 Fee (per candidate) |
|---|---|
| Invaio Sciences | **$200K** |
| Innatrix | **$100K** |

---

### Tier 3 — Regulatory / Registration License
**Trigger:** Partner files for EPA biopesticide registration (biochemical pesticide category) or equivalent international registration for a candidate.

**Rights granted:** Full regulatory submission rights. Partner may include Agros-originated candidate data in EPA registration dossier and own the registration.

**Priced per candidate submitted for registration.**

| Partner | Tier 3 Fee (per candidate) |
|---|---|
| Invaio Sciences | **$400K** |
| Innatrix | **$250K** |

---

### Tier 4 — Commercial License
**Trigger:** Partner achieves first commercial sale of a product containing an Agros-originated candidate.

**Rights granted:** Perpetual commercial rights for the registered product in the agreed territory.

**Structure:** Royalty on net sales, paid quarterly.

| Partner | Royalty Rate |
|---|---|
| Invaio Sciences | **5% of net sales** |
| Innatrix | **4% of net sales** |

## Key Terms to Negotiate

- **Exclusivity scope:** Per-target, per-batch. Agros retains right to generate new batches for different targets and license to others.
- **IP ownership:** All platform IP stays with Agros. Candidates are licensed; Agros may publish or patent the design methodology independently.
- **Sublicensing:** Partner may not sublicense Agros candidates to third parties without a separate agreement.
- **Data rights:** Agros receives aggregated in vivo results from Tier 1 screening (anonymized if needed) — this is the flywheel that improves the model.
- **Batch refresh:** If partner wants a second batch against the same target (after results from Tier 1), that is a new Tier 1 agreement at the same rate.
- **Minimum advance timeline:** Tier 1 exclusivity clock starts at delivery. If partner has not initiated field trials within 24 months, exclusivity lapses.
