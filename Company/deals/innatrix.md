## Company Background

Innatrix is a SOSV-backed peptide biopesticide company based in Research Triangle Park, NC. Peptide platform targeting three major agricultural diseases:
- **InnalB** — potato late blight
- **InnaNema** — soybean cyst nematode
- **InnaHLB** — citrus greening

First product launch targeted 2026. Pre-EPA registration. Flagged by Pam Marrone as relevant to our space.
