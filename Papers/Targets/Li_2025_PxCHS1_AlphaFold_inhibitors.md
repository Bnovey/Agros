# AlphaFold-Assisted Design and Synthesis of V-Type Amidazoles as Chitin Synthase Inhibitors against *Plutella xylostella*

**Authors:** Li Y, Zhang W, Chen X, et al.
**Journal:** *Journal of Agricultural and Food Chemistry*, 2025
**Link:** https://doi.org/10.1021/acs.jafc.5c03138

## Abstract

AlphaFold2 models of PxCHS1 were used for virtual screening and structure-based design of V-type amidazole inhibitors. The glycosyltransferase active site cavity was identified and validated by docking and MD simulation. Several amidazole compounds showed in vitro CHS inhibition (IC50 2–15 µM) and in vivo activity against *P. xylostella* larvae (LC50 8–30 mg/L). Compounds were inactive against mammalian glycosyltransferases, confirming selectivity.
