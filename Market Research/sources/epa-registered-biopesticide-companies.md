## Key Takeaways for Agros

1. **Suterra/Vestaron** is the direct peptide-bioinsecticide incumbent — their spider venom peptide platform (Spear® Lep, Basin) is the closest existing analog to what we're building, but they use known natural sequences, not AI-designed ones.

2. **Biotalys EVOCA** (Dec 2025) is the first protein-based biofungicide to clear EPA — establishes that novel engineered proteins *can* get through the biochemical pesticide pathway. Relevant precedent for our fungal target (*Botrytis cinerea* β-glucan synthase).

3. **Solasta Bio's** Nov 2024 "biochemical-like" classification is the most relevant regulatory signal: EPA is actively categorizing insect neuropeptide disruptors into a lower-burden pathway. Our lepidopteran nAChR peptides would likely qualify for the same track.

4. **PI AgSciences** (PREtec™, March 2026) shows an AI-adjacent peptide platform company is actively commercializing — worth monitoring as a potential partner or comp.
