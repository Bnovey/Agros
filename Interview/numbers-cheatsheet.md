## Anchors to Memorize (the ones that land in conversation)

- **"$10 billion"** — Botrytis annual losses. Say it early.
- **"94%"** — resistance rate to the most common fungicide. The number that makes growers' problem visceral.
- **"$7–8 billion growing at 12–15%"** — biopesticide market. Have this as one phrase.
- **"2 years"** — EPA biochemical biopesticide registration timeline. Fast compared to conventional.
- **"Ea peptide 91398"** — the EPA-registered peptide precedent. Proves the pathway works.
- **"$480K non-dilutive"** — what USDA + NSF SBIR can add on top of YC.
- **"Bindwell raised $6M and screens blindly"** — the one-line competitive differentiation.
