# YC Interview Prep

YC interviews are ~10 minutes, rapid-fire. Partners interrupt constantly. Every answer must be ≤ 30 seconds and land a clear point.

## Interview Format

- 10 minutes total, ~15–20 questions
- 2–4 YC partners in the room
- They will cut you off — brevity is survival
- They will probe inconsistencies — know your numbers cold
- End with a clear ask: what you need, what you'll do with it
