# YC Interview Drill Questions

Ordered by danger level. Practice 1–5 until they're automatic — if you stumble on those, nothing else matters.

---

## The Killers (hardest, most likely)

1. Have you talked to any customers?
2. Have you done any wet lab validation?
3. YC already funded Bindwell — why are you different?
4. Why are you the right people to build this? You're undergrads.
5. What have you actually built?

---

## Traction / Progress

6. What will you do with the $500K?
7. What's your 3-month milestone?
8. How long until you have in vitro data?
9. Where are you doing your wet lab work?
10. How many peptide candidates have you generated?

---

## Technical

11. How does BoltzGen work?
12. What is Boltz-2 and what does it output?
13. What are Integrated Gradients and why do they matter?
14. Why not just use AlphaFold?
15. How do you know your IG analysis is correct?
16. What's your hit rate going to be?
17. How is this different from docking-based approaches like AutoDock Vina?
18. What if Boltz-2 is wrong about your agricultural targets — it was trained on pharma data?

---

## Market

19. How big is the market?
20. Who is your customer — the grower or the agchem company?
21. Why biopesticides now?
22. What's the regulatory path to market?
23. Why Botrytis specifically?

---

## Business Model

24. How do you make money?
25. When do you have your first dollar of revenue?
26. Why would Bayer or BASF license from you instead of building this themselves?
27. What's the licensing deal structure?

---

## Competition

28. What about big foundation models — can't OpenAI or Google just do this?
29. What if Bindwell adds interpretability?
30. What do the traditional biopesticide companies (Certis, BioWorks) do and why aren't they doing this?

---

## Team

31. What does each of you do?
32. Are you both full-time?
33. How did you meet?
34. What's the hardest thing you've built together?

---

## Hard / Gotcha

35. What could kill this company?
36. Why hasn't this been done before?
37. What don't you know that you should?
38. What's your plan if your first 20 peptides don't work in the wet lab?
39. What do you want from YC beyond money?
